Hi 👋 My name is Jesudara Jeminusi
==================================

Blockchain and Backend Developer
--------------------------------

As a blockchain developer, My primary responsibility is to help build decentralised applications on EVM-compatible networks and have them tested and deployed. I also major in developing enterprise blockchain solutions with enterprise frameworks like Hyperledger fabric and besu. As a backend developer, I am familiar with building APIS (REST and Graphql) with nodesjs and golang

* 🌍  I'm based in Abuja
* ✉️  You can contact me at [jesudara.j@gmail.com](mailto:jesudara.j@gmail.com)
* 🚀  I'm currently working on [CHATS](http://chats.cash/)
* 🧠  I'm learning hyperledger fabric and improving my skills on golang
* 🤝  I'm open to collaborating on enterprise-blockchain projects
* ⚡  An Arsenal fan ⚽️
